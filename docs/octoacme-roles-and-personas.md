# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## QA/Testing Specialists

### Role Summary
QA/Testing Specialists validate that delivered work meets quality standards, acceptance criteria, and user expectations. They design test strategies, execute tests, and identify defects before release.

### Responsibilities
- Design and execute unit, integration, and end-to-end test plans
- Define acceptance criteria in collaboration with Product and Development teams
- Execute manual QA for features and regressions
- Document test results and defect reports
- Participate in release validation and smoke testing
- Provide feedback on testability during design and development reviews
- Maintain test automation infrastructure and scripts

### Goals
- Ensure product quality and reliability before release
- Reduce production defects and support incidents
- Enable faster iteration cycles with effective automation
- Maintain comprehensive test coverage for critical user flows

### Typical Communication
- Test plan reviews with Product and Development
- Defect reports and quality metrics in standups
- Release readiness assessments
- Test results and QA sign-off for deployments

---

## Technical Leads

### Role Summary
Technical Leads provide technical direction and architecture guidance for projects. They mentor team members, ensure technical quality, and manage dependencies and integration challenges.

### Responsibilities
- Design technical architecture and validate feasibility
- Guide design decisions and code quality standards
- Mentor and support developers on complex technical challenges
- Identify technical risks and propose mitigation strategies
- Facilitate code reviews and technical discussions
- Interface with other technical teams on dependencies and integration
- Contribute to technology decisions and tool evaluations

### Goals
- Deliver scalable, maintainable technical solutions
- Build team technical capability and knowledge sharing
- Manage technical debt and system complexity
- Ensure alignment with organizational standards and best practices

### Typical Communication
- Technical design reviews and architecture discussions
- Code review guidance and mentoring
- Technical risk escalations and dependency coordination
- Engineering sync participation

---

## Project Sponsors

### Role Summary
Project Sponsors provide executive oversight and business alignment. They ensure projects deliver business value, secure resources, and remove organizational blockers at the leadership level.

### Responsibilities
- Approve project initiation and funding
- Define business value and strategic alignment
- Remove organizational obstacles and resource constraints
- Provide executive oversight and governance
- Approve scope changes and trade-off decisions
- Escalate and resolve cross-organizational dependencies
- Communicate project status and outcomes to leadership

### Goals
- Ensure projects deliver measurable business value
- Maintain strategic alignment with organizational priorities
- Reduce project risk through executive engagement
- Enable team success by removing systemic blockers

### Typical Communication
- Project initiation and approval gate reviews
- Monthly stakeholder updates and business impact reports
- Escalation path for blockers and decisions requiring leadership approval
- Post-project retrospectives and lessons learned

---

## Stakeholders

### Role Summary
Stakeholders are individuals and teams impacted by or interested in project outcomes. They include customers, internal teams, partners, and support functions. Stakeholders provide input, validation, and accountability for project success.

### Responsibilities
- Provide domain expertise and user perspectives
- Validate solutions meet their needs and requirements
- Communicate project impact and changes to their teams
- Provide feedback on features, usability, and quality
- Support user adoption and implementation
- Escalate concerns and constraints that affect project success

### Goals
- Ensure delivered solutions meet stakeholder needs and expectations
- Reduce surprises and alignment gaps through active involvement
- Drive adoption and successful implementation
- Provide feedback that improves product-market fit

### Typical Communication
- Kickoff and planning sessions to provide input
- Regular demos and review sessions for feedback
- Status updates and change notifications
- Post-release communication and support coordination

---

## How these personas are used in the exercise
- Use these persona definitions to frame scenarios and sample interactions in the Skills Exercise.
- Each persona can be used as a persona prompt for Copilot Spaces to shape role-specific guidance.
- When projects cross multiple functions, ensure clear assignment of responsibilities and communication paths aligned with these persona definitions.
